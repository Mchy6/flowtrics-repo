What is workflow automation?
Workflow automation refers to the use of software to complete tasks and activities without the need for human input. It is a tool that makes work faster, easier, and more consistent by automating repetitive tasks and reducing the need for manual work. Workflow automation also plays a crucial role in process orchestration.
