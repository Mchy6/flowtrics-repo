Built for any Work Environment
Welcome to Flowtrics, your all-in-one project management solution designed to help your team reach its full potential in any industry or department.  With a focus on user experience, advanced AI Data analysis and document management, Task Workflow Automation, and seamless form capabilities, Flowtrics is the perfect choice for businesses of all sizes.
Innovative Document Management with AI Technology
Flowtrics stands out from other project management platforms with its innovative approach to document management. By leveraging AI-powered features such as document analysis, form analysis, and PDF automation, Flowtrics streamlines workflow processes and reduces the time and resources spent on manual data entry.
Transform Your Document and Form Handling Process
With Flowtrics, managing documents and forms for any project is no longer tedious. Our AI technology allows for advanced document and form analysis, making document handling more efficient and accurate.  Say goodbye to manual data entry and hello to streamlined workflows that save you time and resources.
Automation for Increased Productivity
Flowtrics takes document management a step further with its automation capabilities. With features like web forms and PDF document filling, you can automate repetitive tasks and free up valuable time to focus on more critical aspects of your business. This increases productivity, reduces the chances of errors, and improves overall workflow.
Custom Form Creation and Automation
Flowtrics empowers users to create custom forms tailored to their specific needs, thus facilitating an even deeper level of automation within the task process. This feature enables teams to efficiently collect the data necessary for initiating various workflows, from project initiation documents to client feedback forms. Once a form is submitted, Flowtrics’s advanced automation tools can extract and deploy this data to automatically populate other forms, update systems, or even trigger subsequent processes. This seamless integration and automation capability ensures that data flows effortlessly between tasks and processes, reducing manual intervention and enhancing overall efficiency and accuracy in project management.

Custom PDF Document Creation, Automatic Filling, and E-Signing
Flowtrics revolutionizes how businesses manage projects with their documents with custom PDF document creation, automatic filling, and electronic signing features. This powerful trio of capabilities simplifies the generation and processing of business documents, contracts, and agreements. Users can easily design custom PDFs tailored to their specific requirements, automate the filling of these documents with relevant data captured through forms or existing databases, and expedite the review and approval process with secure e-signature functionality. This eliminates the need for manual data entry, significantly reduces errors, and streamlines workflows, allowing agreements to be executed more quickly and efficiently. With Flowtrics, you can ensure that your document management process is faster and compliant with legal standards, offering unparalleled convenience and security.
Efficient Resource Allocation
With Flowtrics, you can better use your resources by reducing the time and effort spent on repetitive tasks, data implementation, and document management. By automating tasks and streamlining workflows, your team can focus on more critical projects and tasks, increasing efficiency and productivity. This efficient resource management is more cost-effective and allows for a more accurate allocation of resources.
Customizable Workflows for Maximum Efficiency
Flowtrics offers customizable workflows tailored to fit the unique needs of your team. With our intuitive workflow builder, you can design and automate processes specific to your business, eliminating unnecessary steps and ensuring maximum efficiency.
Kanban Views for Visual Workflow Management
Easily track progress and manage tasks with Flowtrics’ Kanban views. This visual representation of your workflows allows for a more intuitive and organized approach to managing projects. By providing a clear overview of tasks, deadlines, and progress, Kanban views help teams stay on track and achieve their goals.
Collaboration Made Easy
With Flowtrics’ customizable workflows, collaboration becomes seamless. Team members can easily communicate and collaborate on tasks, assign responsibilities, and track progress all in one place. This improves communication and ensures everyone is on the same page, leading to more efficient and successful project completion.

External Portals: Bridging the Gap for Non-Users
Flowtrics introduces external portals, a game-changing feature designed to extend collaboration and communication beyond the confines of your organization. These portals enable non-user stakeholders, such as clients, vendors, or external partners, to interact with your team by sharing documents, tasks, forms, and more. Through these secure, user-friendly interfaces, external parties can submit information, sign documents, participate in chats, and receive notifications without needing full access to the Flowtrics system. This elevation in collaboration ensures that all involved parties are coordinated and fully informed, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of your projects and processes. External portals represent another step toward comprehensive project management, making Flowtrics a tool for your team and a hub for all your business interactions.

Whether it’s connecting your email, payroll, EHRs, or other project management tools, Flowtrics makes it easy to access all your necessary data in one place. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
How does Flowtrics ensure the security of our documents?
Flowtrics employs advanced security measures, including end-to-end encryption, secure e-signature technology, and compliance with global legal standards to ensure your documents are always protected. Our platform is designed to provide a high level of security that meets the needs of businesses in various industries.
Can I customize workflows to fit my unique business processes?
Yes, Flowtrics offers a customizable workflow builder that allows you to tailor workflows to fit the specific needs of your business. You can create, automate, and modify processes to eliminate unnecessary steps and ensure that your team operates efficiently.
Is Flowtrics suitable for businesses of all sizes?
Absolutely! Flowtrics is designed to scale with your business. Whether you’re a small startup or a large enterprise, our platform can be tailored to meet your needs, helping you manage tasks, collaborate, and streamline workflows efficiently.
How do external portals work, and how can they benefit my business?
External portals allow stakeholders outside your organization, such as clients, vendors, or partners, to collaborate with your team without needing full access to the Flowtrics platform. They can share documents, complete forms, sign agreements, and participate in discussions. This enhances communication, improves project efficiency, and ensures that all parties are aligned.
What kind of customer support can I expect from Flowtrics?
At Flowtrics, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer comprehensive support through various channels, including email, live chat, and phone. Our dedicated support team is available to assist with any queries or issues you might encounter, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.
Will Flowtrics integrate with the tools we are currently using?
Yes, Flowtrics offers seamless integration with various tools and platforms, including email, payroll systems, EHRs, and other project management tools. This allows you to streamline your workflows and access all your necessary data in one place, enhancing productivity and saving time.
What steps is Flowtrics taking towards continuous innovation?
Our team is constantly developing new features and improving existing ones to stay ahead of the curve in project management technology. We listen to our users’ feedback and keep abreast of the latest trends to ensure our platform meets the evolving needs of businesses across industries.
